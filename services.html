Comprehensive Care Coordination

Seeking medical treatment abroad can be overwhelming. We remove the stress, confusion, and uncertainty, allowing you to focus entirely on recovery.

Medical Record Coordination

We securely collect, organize, and share your medical history and reports with partner hospitals to ensure doctors have the information they need.

Hospital Communication

We act as the liaison, scheduling consultations, managing time zones, and ensuring smooth dialogue between you and the medical team.

Treatment & Cost Coordination

We help you understand the hospital-provided treatment plans and cost estimates, ensuring clarity before you travel.

Payment Facilitation

We assist with the authorized collection and transfer of medical payments to partner hospitals, streamlining the financial process.

Travel & Visa Guidance

We assist with the necessary documentation, including medical visa invitation letters, and provide guidance for travel planning.

Accommodation & Logistics

We coordinate comfortable stays near the hospital and arrange for reliable local transportation during your visit.

On-Ground Support

Our India operations team provides local assistance, greeting you upon arrival and being available for on-site support.

Post-Treatment Follow-Up

We facilitate the sharing of post-discharge reports and help coordinate remote communication after you return home.

Important Scope of Service

To maintain ethical standards and patient safety, please understand what we do not do:

  • We do not provide medical diagnosis.
  • We do not offer treatment recommendations.
  • We do not guarantee medical outcomes.
  • We are not a substitute for professional medical advice.
