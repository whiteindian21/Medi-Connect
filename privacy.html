Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Medi Connect International, we understand the sensitivity of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with South African data protection laws, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Introduction

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your information. We view data privacy not just as a legal obligation, but as a fundamental part of the ethical care we provide.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website, email communications, and physical forms.

2. Information Collected

To provide effective coordination services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, contact numbers, email address, residential address, and country of origin.
  • Medical Information: Medical reports, diagnosis documents, scan results (X-rays, MRIs), treatment history, pathology reports, and current medications.
  • Travel & Identification Information: Passport copies, visa details, flight schedules, and travel insurance information.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information strictly for the purpose of facilitating your medical journey:

  • Medical Coordination: To share your medical history with partner hospitals (e.g., Thangam Cancer Center) to secure appointments and treatment plans.
  • Communication: To keep you and your family informed about appointments, itinerary changes, and logistical updates.
  • Payment Facilitation: To process invoices and manage financial transactions on behalf of hospitals.
  • Travel & Logistics: To book flights, accommodations, and arrange airport transfers.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data to third parties. However, to facilitate your care, we may share your information with:

  • Partner Hospitals: Medical information is shared with the treating hospital and doctors in India.
  • Authorized Agents: Travel agencies, visa facilitation agents, or accommodation providers strictly as needed for your trip.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law to comply with valid legal processes.
Consent Requirement: We will only share your medical records with third parties (including hospitals) with your explicit prior consent.

5. Cross-Border Data Transfers

As Medi Connect International facilitates medical travel to India, your personal and medical information will be transferred outside of the Republic of South Africa.

We ensure that reasonable security measures are in place to protect your data during these transfers. We require our international partners to adhere to strict confidentiality standards equivalent to those required by South African law.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encryption: Using secure channels (SSL/TLS) for all data transmission over the internet.
  • Restricted Access: Only authorized personnel who require access to perform their duties (e.g., case coordinators) are granted access to your data.
  • Secure Storage: Storing digital records on secure, password-protected systems with firewalls.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law (e.g., for accounting or medical legal purposes).

Once the retention period expires, we will securely destroy or anonymize your records unless you consent to us keeping them for future medical follow-ups.

8. User Rights

In accordance with POPIA, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct or update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or medical retention requirements.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal information.

9. Consent

By using Medi Connect International’s services and submitting your information to us, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, processing, and transfer of your data as described herein.
