How We Help You

We handle the logistics and coordination so you can focus entirely on healing.

Medical Record Management

We securely collect, digitize, and transfer your medical history to the specialists at Thangam Cancer Center for pre-arrival review.

Visa & Travel Logistics

From medical visa invitation letters to booking flights and airport transfers, we ensure a smooth arrival in India.

Doctor Communication

We facilitate tele-consultations before you travel and act as a bridge between you and the medical team during your stay.

Accommodation & Support

We arrange comfortable guest houses or hotels near the hospital and provide local language support during your visit.

Your Journey to Recovery

A transparent, step-by-step pathway coordinated by Medi Connect.

Step 1: Initial Inquiry

Contact us via email or form. We schedule a confidential call to understand your medical condition and needs.

Step 2: Case Review

Submit your reports. Our medical liaison at Thangam Cancer Center reviews them to provide an initial treatment plan estimate.

Step 3: Travel Planning

Once you approve, we assist with the Medical Visa and book your travel and accommodation.

Step 4: Arrival & Treatment

Our team receives you at the airport. You are admitted to Thangam Cancer Center for treatment with full translation support.
